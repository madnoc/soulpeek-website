Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Overview

SoulPeek ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the SoulPeek mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account information: When you create an account, we collect your email address (or Apple/Google account identifier), display name, birth date, and optionally your birth time and birth place. This information is used to personalize your readings.

Reading data: When you request a reading, we process the information you provide (questions, photos, preferences) to generate your reading. This data is stored securely and associated with your account.

Photos: If you upload photos for coffee cup, palm, face, or aura readings, these photos are stored temporarily and automatically deleted after 7 days. Photos are transmitted securely and processed only for the purpose of generating your reading.

Usage data: We collect anonymous usage analytics (which features are used, reading types requested) to improve the app experience. We use PostHog for analytics, which does not collect personally identifiable information.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve the SoulPeek service: generating personalized readings, maintaining your reading history, calculating your zodiac sign and numerological profile, and sending notifications you have opted into.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit. We use row-level security to ensure you can only access your own data. Photos are stored in private, access-controlled storage buckets.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate SoulPeek:

Supabase (database, authentication, storage), Anthropic Claude API (reading generation, your data is processed according to Anthropic's data usage policy and is not used for model training), RevenueCat (in-app purchase management), PostHog (anonymous analytics), Firebase (push notifications).

Data Retention

Photos: Automatically deleted after 7 days unless you choose to save them. Reading history: Retained as long as your account is active. Account data: Deleted within 30 days of account deletion request.

Your Rights

You have the right to: access all your data (via the "Download My Data" feature in Settings), delete your account and all associated data, opt out of push notifications at any time, request information about how your data is processed.

Children's Privacy

SoulPeek is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through the app or via email.

Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or data requests, contact us at privacy@soulpeek.app.
