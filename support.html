Frequently Asked Questions

How do credits work?

Credits are used to unlock readings. Different reading types cost different amounts (2 or 3 credits). Your daily horoscope is always free. You can purchase credit packs anytime from the app.

Can I get a refund?

In-app purchases are processed by Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com or contact Apple Support directly.

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to the credit purchase screen in the app and tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom. This will restore any previously purchased credits linked to your Apple ID.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile, then Settings, then tap "Delete Account." All your data, reading history, and photos will be permanently removed within 30 days.

How do I download my data?

Go to Profile, then Settings, then tap "Download My Data." A JSON file containing all your personal data and reading history will be generated.

Are the readings real predictions?

SoulPeek readings are symbolic interpretations rooted in ancient wisdom traditions. They are intended for personal reflection and entertainment, not as factual predictions or professional advice.

What happens to my photos?

Photos uploaded for readings (coffee cup, palm, face, aura) are stored securely and automatically deleted after 7 days. They are only used to generate your reading and are never shared.

How do I change my guide?

Tap the guide chip on the home screen or go to Profile and tap "Change Guide." You can switch between all five guides at any time for free.
